Topic: conveyor belt alignment
Description:
This document describes mechanical aspects of conveyor belt alignment in a manufacturing environment.
Key Parameters:
- Operating speed: 914 RPM
- Torque: 62 Nm
- Temperature limit: 81 °C

Common Failure Modes:
- Misalignment
- Insufficient lubrication
- Overloading

Recommended Actions:
- Follow torque specs
- Verify alignment within 0.005 in
- Inspect bearings regularly
