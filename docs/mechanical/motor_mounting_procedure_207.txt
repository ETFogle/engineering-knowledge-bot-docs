Topic: motor mounting procedure
Description:
This document describes mechanical aspects of motor mounting procedure in a manufacturing environment.
Key Parameters:
- Operating speed: 765 RPM
- Torque: 225 Nm
- Temperature limit: 69 °C

Common Failure Modes:
- Misalignment
- Insufficient lubrication
- Overloading

Recommended Actions:
- Follow torque specs
- Verify alignment within 0.005 in
- Inspect bearings regularly
